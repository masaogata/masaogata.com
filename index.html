  • Masa Ogata is a Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and AI researcher at AIST, where he works on human interface, interaction technologies and intelligent system. He received a Ph.D in Computer Science from Keio University in March, 2016. Prior to graduation, he worked as a research intern at Intelligent Robotics and Communication Lab., ATR and Microsoft Research Asia. He gained academic recognition such as Microsoft Research Asia Fellowship 2013 and ACM CHI 2015 Best Paper Honorable Mention. ACM, IPSJ member. SIGCHI Japan Chapter Boarding member.
